Reefer Monitoring Concerns and Risks

Concerns & Risks
  • Risks of incorrect temperature settings

    Too many containers to manually compare settings provided in the reefer manifest and Baplie for each container.
  • Risks of improper monitoring

    Not highlighting containers with an error code, and not informing the electrician to check. Large financial claims.
  • Inadequate container information

    Not able to compare daily temp against previous voyage reports at the container location.
  • Time consuming

    Crew have to type out all the temperature data and prepare reports, once or twice daily.
